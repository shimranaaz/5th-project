Calling and messaging. The majority of smartwatches allow you to make phone calls. Even some smartwatches have an integrated eSIM feature to make calls even if your smartphone isn't with you. You can send or using a smartwatch.

While your smartwatch will use data to make calls, send texts and receive messages you do not need to have a separate data plan for your smartwatch. For example, a Samsung smartwatch has an eSIM which is paired to your phone's data plan during the setup process..

A smartwatch is a digital watch with its own operating system that can be extended with apps to provide new functions. Development of smartwatches began in the 2000s, with the Apple Watch making its major retail breakthrough in 2014.
